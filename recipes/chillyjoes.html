Chilly Joes

If Chili and Sloppy Joe had a Baby...

...We would get Chilly Joes. This is a poor man's recipe that I came up with while trying to save money.

So buckle up, you are in for an explosion of flavors.

Ingredients:

  • Can of Sloppy Joe Sause
  • 2 Cans of Chili
  • (OPTIONAL) Baked Tator Tots

Steps:

  1. Mix cans of chili and sloppy joe sause in pan
  2. Heat ingredients to desired temperature
  3. Put over optional ingredient
  4. ENJOY
