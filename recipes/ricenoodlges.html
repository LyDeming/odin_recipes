Rice Noodles

Asian Dish

Another delightful poor man's recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Packet of SUPER THIN Rice Noodles
  • Bag of Frozen Peas and Carrots
  • 1 lb Beef
  • Soy Sauce

Steps:

  1. Cook beef in frying pan with peas and carrots
  2. Follow instructions on packet of noodles and cook
  3. Once everything is cooked, mix all the ingredients and add soy sauce to taste
  4. ENJOY
